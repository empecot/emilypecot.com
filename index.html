A little bit about me.

My journey into tech has been a circuitous one. I believe they call it a "non-linear career path." But certain traits have been a constant throughout my career--attention to detail, a highly attuned aesthetic eye, the ability to work in a diverse array of environments and the desire to tell people's stories.

As I was searching for a career that would allow me freedom and versatility, I came upon front-end development and had an epiphany. The more I explored web design and coding, the more excited I became about the possibilities for helping other people express themselves and their work.

Now I’ve made a full switch to front-end development, where I can use my organization skills and eye for detail to write clean, elegant code.

Hand-Coded HTML

My focus is writing well-structured and semantic HTML5 by hand, allowing for easy to read content and streamlined collaboration.

Well-Organized CSS

Tidy and lean CSS is my passion. Using the latest best practices, my goal is writing CSS that is lightning fast and easy to build on.

Beautiful and Modern Design

With an eye for color and typefaces, I pride myself on designing websites with style and accurately convert it into a webpage that is pixel-perfect.
